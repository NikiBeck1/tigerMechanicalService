Built on Trust,
Driven by Results

Tiger Mechanical Service was founded by Hunter Edwards with a simple belief: people deserve honest service at a fair price. Since opening in 2023, Hunter and his team have built one of Birmingham's most-recommended HVAC operations, entirely through word of mouth and 5-star reviews.

Every technician arrives on time. Every price is quoted up front. Every job site is left cleaner than they found it. And every repair is backed by a 90-day minimum warranty, because they stand behind their work.
